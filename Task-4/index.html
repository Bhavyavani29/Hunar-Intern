About BMI

What is BMI?
BMI (Body Mass Index) is a value calculated from your height and weight to estimate body fat and health status.

Formulas:

  • Metric: BMI = weight (kg) / height (m²)
  • US: BMI = (weight (lbs) / height (in)²) × 703

BMI Categories:

  • Under 18.5 → Underweight
  • 18.5–24.9 → Normal
  • 25.0–29.9 → Overweight
  • 30.0 and above → Obese

⚠️ Limitations:

  • Doesn’t differentiate muscle and fat
  • May not be accurate for all ages/body types
  • Ignores Other Health Factors
  • Not Accurate for All Ages
